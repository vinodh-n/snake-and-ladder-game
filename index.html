How to Play

Objective

Be the first player to reach position 100 on the board.

Game Rules

  1. Rolling the Dice: Players take turns rolling a virtual dice (1-6)
  2. Moving: Move your piece forward by the number shown on the dice
  3. Ladders: If you land on a ladder's bottom, climb up to its top
  4. Snakes: If you land on a snake's head, slide down to its tail
  5. Exact Number: You must land exactly on 100 to win. If you roll too high, you stay at your current position
  6. Winning: First player to reach 100 wins the game!

Board Features

  • 🪜 Ladders (Green): Climb up to advance faster
  • 🐍 Snakes (Red): Slide down - be careful!
  • 🔵 Player 1 (Blue): First player marker
  • 🟡 Player 2 (Yellow): Second player marker

Tips

  • Plan your moves carefully to avoid snake tiles
  • Try to land on ladder tiles when possible
  • Keep track of your opponent's position
  • The dice value is random, so luck plays a big role!
